Ramoji Film city, Hyderabad Overview

A place that is perfect for all the lovers of cinema and Bollywood! Set up by Ramoji Rao, the head of Ramoji group in 1991, Ramoji Film City is a spectacular getaway a little outside the city of Hyderabad. It is spread over 2500 acres and has been certified as the World's largest Film Studio Complex by the Guinness Book of World Records. Ramoji Film City can accommodate 20 film units at one go and has various shooting locations including London Street, Hollywood signage, Japanese gardens, airport, hospital, landscapes and buildings as well as laboratories. Being one of the most popular places to visit in Hyderabad, one can reach Ramoji Film City through a number of packaged tours and buses that leave regularly from the city.

Ramoji Film City is a fun and diverse venue where you can indulge in a number of activities ranging from film city tours to adventure sports and toy train rides to a number of gardens. Smaller renditions of famous monuments in the country such as the Amer Palace are also present here. What's more, you can also stroll through a number of other sets and settings such as royal courts and other locations that have drawn inspiration from different architectures throughout the world. If you decide to visit this destination, after all, be sure to keep aside an entire day for this fun-filled location!

Things to do at Ramoji Film city

1. Adventure sports: The film city promotes sports like Bungee ejection, Football, swinging and climbing through the loop, basketball and cricket among many other.
2. Studio tour: In this tour, you can go for a tour of the whole studio and can also visit the famous Askari Garden, Japanese Garden, Mughal Garden, Sun Fountain Garden, Angel's Fountain Garden, Sanctuary Garden and many more.
3. Movie magic park: At the Movie Magic Park of the Ramoji Film City, there are sudden earthquake tremors, you can enjoy simulation experience of free fall, amazing acoustic effects, Filmi Duniya and Action Studio if you are a movie lover this is just perfect for you.
4. The birds park called "Wings": Love watching birds or studying them? At Wings, there are a variety of exotic bird species like Green napped Lorikeet, Yellow-streaked Lorry, Swainson Lory etc. If you enjoy spotting birds in their natural surroundings, this is just the place for you.
5. Kid's park: The famous kid's park is filled with adventurous and fun rides that everyone can enjoy to the heart's fullest content. From swing sets to slides, a number of such facilities are available here, which will keep your kids entertained for hours. 
6. Live shows: Love theatre? Live shows consist of stunts, drama, music and dance are performed by professional artists who do it with perfection. The thrill of live performances is one of the best things to witness here.
7. Eureka: If you are a lover of history and wish to travel down the ancient historical times and experience the Mughal Period or the majestic Mauryans time, you must visit Eureka. You will be left awestruck by the magnificent displays of national as well as international cultural artefacts. The sheer scale and regalia of this place will leave you awestruck.

Layout of Ramoji City

The expansive area Ramoji City has a number of assorted activities within its bounds. As soon as you enter the city, a replica of London bus picks you right up and drops you in a town that resembles the Texas country side. Once you've had your fill of the United States, you can stroll over the Eiffel Tower and Japanese - roofed buildings that lie closeby. If you haven't had the pleasure of visiting the Amer Palace, you can definitely take a taste of the original thing here at the Amer - palace inspired edifice. Mesmerizing Kalbelia dance performance coupled with melodious music will transport you straight to Rajasthan. Next, you can hop straight into the train that takes you through 'Filmy Dunia', a labyrinth of tunnels that take you from snow - capped mountains to sandy deserts, and is especially delightful to children. Ramoji City also has a huge garden area where you can relax and enjoy in the lap of nature after a long day of fun and frolic.

Best Time To Visit Ramoji Film city

The best time to visit Ramoji Film City is during winter as Hyderabad gets very hot during summers. You should visit Ramoji Film City from October to February, when the weather is cool and at its best.

Tips For Visiting Ramoji Film city

1. Wear comfortable walking shoes
2. Carry water bottles, sunscreen, a hat and sunglasses as it can get pretty hot here
3. Beware of monkeys in the garden areas

How To Reach Ramoji Film city

Local transport facilities are available and easy to get. The nearest railway station to ramoji film city is Secunderabad Jn Train Station which is 22 km away from Ramoji Film City. But if you are travelling in your own vehicle, take the straight road on the Vijayawada Highway from the main city. With a little help from your GPS or the local people, you will reach Ramoji Film city within an hour.
