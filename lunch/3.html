NAME:Cream Cheese & Veggie Roll-Up

CALORIES INTAKE

        
        318 Calories

HEALTH PROFITS 

        Rich in calcium: Cream cheese is a good source of calcium, which is 
        essential for strong bones and teeth. High in protein: The protein in 
        cream cheese can help build and repair muscles, making it a great 
        post-workout snack. Contains vitamin A: Vitamin A is important for 
        maintaining healthy skin and vision

MAKING

        Combine cream cheese, chives, dill and garlic powder in a small bowl. 
        Spread evenly on tortilla. Layer spinach, then shredded (or sliced) 
        vegetables across the bottom third of the tortilla. Roll closed and cut 
        in half. Or, cut into slices, if desired.
