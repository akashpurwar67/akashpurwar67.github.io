RECIPE NAME:Cucumber Sandwich

COLORIES INTAKE

        250 calories. 10g protein. 10g fat.

HEALTH BENIFITS

        Thanks to their water content, cucumbers can add to your hydration 
        status. They contain the antioxidant silica, which plays an important 
        role in collagen synthesis and keeping your skin smooth and firm. There 
        is also evidence that suggests the compounds in cucumbers may help 
        prevent cancer and diabetes.

RECIPE MAKING

        Stir cream cheese, yogurt, chives, dill and pepper together in a small
        bowl until well blended. Spread the mixture evenly on one side of each 
        bread slice. Top 1 slice with cucumber slices, then top with the other 
        bread slice, cream cheese-side down. Cut the crusts from the sandwich 
        and cut it in half diagonally.
